## Pronombres personales
- I -> yo
- You -> tu, usted/es
- He -> el (tilde)
- She -> ella
- It -> eso, esa, ese (un solo objetos)
- We -> nosotros
- They -> ellos
## Verbo To Be
*ser o estar* (am - are -is)
- am -> I 
----- I am in class (*estoy*) 
----- I am a teacher (*soy*)
- are -> They, We, You 
----- Juan and Carmen (*They*) are friends
- is -> She, He, It
----- Emmanuel (*He*) is my brother
